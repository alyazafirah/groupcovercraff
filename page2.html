FAQ

leave your curiosity in chats !!

What happens after an order is made?

You will receive an email confirmation on your purchase and be provided with an Order Number.
The order will be processed within 2 business days upon payment verification, and an email
with the tracking details will be sent once the order is successfully shipped. 

How can I track my order?

Our staff will provide you the tracking number after the shipment has been done.

Can I add/remove/change items upon order successfully placed?

No changes can be made once an order has been placed. We highly recommend that you
review your order before proceeding to checkout with our staff. 

Can I cancel my order?

Yes, you can request for order cancellation but it will be subject to the discretion of our team,
depending on your cancellation reason (e.g.: duplicated order). 

What payment options do you accept on cover craft?

We accept online transfer payment, Boost, GrabPay, and more via our payment gateway
service providers. A full list of payment method options will be displayed upon checking out with
our staff.

Do you provide Cash on Delivery (COD)?

No, we do not facilitate COD service at the moment.
